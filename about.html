About the Founder

Samuel Amankwah Arhin is the visionary founder of Scal AI, a revolutionary food scanning application designed to help people track their nutrition and make healthier food choices.

With a passion for technology and health, Samuel created Scal AI to bridge the gap between nutrition awareness and everyday eating habits. The app leverages cutting-edge AI technology to provide instant nutritional information through simple photo capture.

Samuel believes that everyone deserves access to nutritional information, and Scal AI is his contribution to making healthy eating accessible to all.
